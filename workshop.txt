title=Neurodiverse Traits
description=This mods adds Neurodiverse Traits as negative traits:
description=
description=[b]ADHD - Cost +7[/b]
description=- Everyday you have a 35% chance to be in Hyperfocus, but will otherwise be in Hypofocus. 40% chance with the Lucky trait
description=- Hyperfocus will grant you the traits: Fast Learner, Fast Reader, Organized, and Dextrous.
description=- Hypofocus will grant you the traits: Slow Learner, Slow Reader, Disorganized and All Thumbs.
description=- Each survived day Increases the chance of Hyperfocus by 0.5% Up to a max cap of 70%.
description=- Medication: Adderal - Negates all positive and negative effects of ADHD untill the next roll (00:00AM). Best used in the morning, starts with 20 pills
description=
description=[b]Bipolar I Disorder - Cost +7[/b]
description=- Everyday you switch between Bipolar Mania and Bipolar Depression.
description=- Bipolar Mania grants you the following buffs: Sadness reduction, Stress reduction, Endurance boost, as well as the  Wakeful trait.
description=- Bipolar Depression grants you the following De-buffs: Sadness increase, Stress increase, Endurance decrease, and the Sleepyhead trait.
description=- Note: I'm aware that this is supposed to be a weekly switch, but this first version is daily.
description=
description=[b]Tourette Syndrome - Cost +10[/b]
description=- Every 10 in-game minutes, you will have a 15% chance of having a tic between shouting, shoving, racking, reloading, or attacking.
description=- The chance scales with panic up to +15%, and decreases with survived days, up to -5%
description=
description=[b]Alzheimer's disease - Cost +18[/b]
description=- Everyday each Skill will have 50% chance to lose 2.5% XP, this does not apply to Passive and Agility Skills
description=
